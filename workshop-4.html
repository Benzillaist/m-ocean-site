Workshop 4: 1/3/24

Driving Opportunities for Diversity, Equity,and Excellence throughout the Offshore Wind Workforce Pipeline

Workshop Summary

This conversation brought together U.S. offshore wind industry leaders talking about the evolving understanding of workforce needs beyond construction, and by experts in workforce development/training/mentoring who successfully bring opportunity to career seekers by meeting them where they are with the right support at the right time. The objective was to stir new thinking, partners & models for staffing up the offshore wind industry for long-term sustainability and equity.
