MOCEAN Proposal:

Accelerating a Just Energy Transition While Nurturing Healthy Oceans and New Blue Economies Through Innovative Nature-Inclusive Offshore Wind Farms

Virginia's First Offshore Wind Turbines Have Become A Haven For Marine Life:

After only a couple of years in the water, the two offshore wind monopiles off the coast of Virginia show great promise for the potential of offshore wind foundations and scour protection to strengthen fishing stocks

Nature-Based Designs For Augmenting Offshore Wind Structures:

Turbine Reefs could be created by the underwater infrastructure of offshore wind (foundations and scour protection), and now is the perfect time to be experimenting with potential enhancements

Miami Beach Hybrid Reef Project:

These perforated concrete tubes are expected to lead to the creation of reefs in years that would have taken nature alone to do in decades

Fish Reef Project:

The Fish Reef Project uses kelp, seaweed, and other farms could be located within wind farms to provide food and employment opportunities

Belgian Energy Island:

The European Community has a two-decade head start on the Offshore Renewable Energy, and we have much to learn from their initiatives

New Rules to Protect Whales And Jobs for Offshore Wind Development:

Many "engineering decisions" have a major impact on both the health of ocean habitats and on the economic opportunities across the employment spectrum

New Paper On Gravity-Based Foundations for Offshore Wind Turbines:

The paper "Low-Carbon, Nature-Inclusive Concrete Gravity-Based Foundationsfor Offshore Wind Turbines" covers longer design lives for concrete structures which provide greater opportunity for creating turbine reefs

EU Missions - Restore Our Oceans and Waters:

The European Horizon Program are investing 175 Billion Euros over 14 years in use-inspired research. One of their five missions is the Ocean, and much is being learned through large multi-institutional projects

Impact of Offshore Wind on Biodiversity:

Symposium of U.S. and the international community to discuss how to design ecologically net positive offshore wind farms

Integration of Crucial Data Sources:

Data informed dynamic habitat models are used to inform nature inclusive development of offshore wind farm locations and construction procedures

Offshore Wind Energy Model in the open-source InVEST ecosystem service modeling software

Our Mission Statement

The U.S. is at the cusp of the large-scale development of its offshore wind energy resource along its eastern seaboard. This brings great challenges and opportunities for the shared-use of our coastal waters. MOCEAN (Mission being the OCEAN) is focused on advancing marine and material science, engineering practice, economic models, bluetech, policy, and data-driven frameworks that will ensure healthy oceans, a strong and diverse new blue economy, and a just energy transition.
