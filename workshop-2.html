Workshop 2: 1/6/23

Accelerating A Just Energy Transition While Nurturing Healthy Oceans and New Blue Economies Through Innovative Nature-Inclusive Offshore Wind Farms

Workshop Summary

This workshop presented the proposed “Engine” to NSF’s Regional Innovation Engine program. If successful, this would bring $160M over 10 years to support the nature-inclusive design and operation of offshore wind farms with the purpose of improving marine ecosystems, strengthening fisheries, and advancing blue technologies so to bring substantial opportunities to low-income and disadvantages communities. The presenters at this workshop are key personnel for the proposed Engine.
