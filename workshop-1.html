Workshop 1: 5/16/22

Supporting Marine Ecosystems Through Nature-Inclusive Design

Workshop Summary

This workshop present efforts and observations on how offshore wind energy farms could be designed to support healthy marine ecosystems. Presentations and discussions were led by those who are conducting and synthesizing the result of investigations. These individuals were from NREL, PNNL, Inspire Environmental, Orsted, Wageningen University, Boskalis, DOE, Heriot Watt University, Deltares, and Florida Tech. This offering of this workshop was supported by BOEM and MassCEC.
